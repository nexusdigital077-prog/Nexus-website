ZERO DEPRECIATION

Full claim value, no deductions for age.

A five-year-old appliance is covered the same as a five-month-old one. We don't discount claims for wear that doesn't affect how the device runs.

What is Covered

  • All functional and mechanical breakdowns
  • Electrical malfunctions and wiring defects
  • Internal hardware failures
  • Repairs mirroring the original manufacturer warranty
  • Parts, labor, and transportation fees

What is Not Covered

  • Physical damage (e.g., broken screens, dents)
  • Liquid damage or water submersion
  • Loss, theft, or intentional damage
  • Accessories and consumables (batteries, cables)
  • Pre-existing damages before warranty activation
